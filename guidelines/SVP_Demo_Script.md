# SVP Demo Script: The Proactive Purchase History

**Objective:**
Demonstrate the power of Fusion and prompt-driven UI generation by transforming a static Purchase History page into a dynamic, actionable dashboard for our customers. 

**Audience:** SVP Leadership and cross-functional teams.

**Setup Requirements:**
- Have Fusion open to the Purchase History page.
- Ensure the Order Cards component library page is open in another tab for reference if needed.

---

## Scene 1: Setting the Stage

**SVP Leader:**
"Good morning, everyone. Today, I want to show you the future of our post-purchase experience. Right now, our Purchase History page does a great job at the basics—showing past orders and receipts. But we want to move from a static ledger to a dynamic, actionable hub. And we're going to build that out live, right now, using AI-driven UI generation."

**[Action]** *Leader navigates to the Purchase History page in Fusion.*
* **Estimated Load Time:** ~1-2 seconds (Renders the standard layout: Account Side Nav, Search/Filters, standard Order List).

---

## Scene 2: Driving Urgency and Upsells

**SVP Leader:**
"Let's start with a common scenario. A customer places a curbside grocery order, but suddenly realizes they need it sooner. Instead of making them dig through menus, let's proactively offer an upgrade right on their dashboard."

**[Action]** *Leader selects the Fusion prompt input on the page.*
**[Input Prompt]:** *"Show an active curbside order with a countdown to edit and a 'Get it now' button to upgrade to express delivery."*

**[Action]** *Leader hits generate.*
* **Estimated Load Time:** ~3-4 seconds to generate and render the active curbside order card.

**SVP Leader:**
"And there it is. A contextual card that not only shows their active order but creates a clear, one-click path to upgrade to Express delivery. It creates urgency with the countdown and drives incremental revenue seamlessly."

---

## Scene 3: Integrating Services (Auto Care)

**SVP Leader:**
"Now, Walmart isn't just retail; it's services. Let's look at our Auto Care Centers. Customers shouldn't have to go to a separate app or page to manage their appointments."

**[Action]** *Leader clears the previous prompt and enters the next one.*
**[Input Prompt]:** *"Show a scheduled oil change appointment card with Check in, Reschedule, and View details actions."*

**[Action]** *Leader hits generate.*
* **Estimated Load Time:** ~3-4 seconds to load the Auto Care appointment card.

**SVP Leader:**
"Perfect. Now the customer sees their upcoming oil change right alongside their everyday purchases, complete with quick actions to check in or reschedule. It's fully integrated."

---

## Scene 4: Engagement and Proactive Care

**SVP Leader:**
"But here is where it gets really powerful. We don't just want to show past orders; we want to proactively help customers manage their lives. Let's look at how we can drive engagement with our Auto Care centers by showing them relevant maintenance alerts before they even know they need them."

**[Action]** *Leader enters the next prompt.*
**[Input Prompt]:** *"Show 2 ways to improve Auto Care engagement."*

**[Action]** *Leader hits generate.*
* **Estimated Load Time:** ~3-4 seconds to load the maintenance health and upsell offer cards.

**SVP Leader:**
"Immediately, the customer sees a personalized maintenance health score for their vehicle and a targeted, member-priced upsell offer for a tire rotation. We're moving from a static receipt to a proactive service dashboard."

---

## Scene 5: The 'One Walmart' Bundle

**SVP Leader:**
"But here is where it gets really powerful. What if that same customer is getting an oil change and also needs to pick up their weekly groceries? We can combine those trips into one seamless experience."

**[Action]** *Leader enters the next prompt.*
**[Input Prompt]:** *"Show a combined card pairing a same-day oil change with a curbside pickup, with a merged bundle total."*

**[Action]** *Leader hits generate.*
* **Estimated Load Time:** ~4-5 seconds to load the combined multi-service card.

**SVP Leader:**
"This is the 'One Walmart' vision in action. A single, unified card that merges their Auto Care appointment with their curbside pickup. One trip, one frictionless experience for the customer."

---

## Scene 6: Proactive Exception Handling

**SVP Leader:**
"Finally, we know things don't always go perfectly. Delays happen. When they do, we shouldn't wait for the customer to call support. We should proactively offer solutions."

**[Action]** *Leader enters the final prompt.*
**[Input Prompt]:** *"Show a delayed delivery warning card with options to reschedule, switch to pickup, or cancel."*

**[Action]** *Leader hits generate.*
* **Estimated Load Time:** ~3-4 seconds to load the delayed delivery warning card.

**SVP Leader:**
"Immediately, the customer sees the delay warning and is given control. They can switch to store pickup right from this card. This reduces support center volume and turns a potential frustration into a moment of trust."

---

## Wrap Up

**SVP Leader:**
"What you just saw took minutes to generate but represents a massive shift in how we serve our customers. We are transforming Purchase History from a simple list into a personalized, proactive, and unified customer dashboard. Thank you."
